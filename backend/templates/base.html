For each song, we also have a popularity score that tracks how popular songs are relative to all other songs. This score ranks all artists and their tracks on a 0 to 100 scale. The popularity index shows how popular a song is on the streaming platform, summing up the popularity of all songs from every artist.

On a scale of 0 to 100, a score of 0 means the track has no popularity, while a score of 100 means the track is among the most popular tracks on the platform. The popularity of a track is calculated by an algorithm and is primarily based on the total number of plays the track has had and how recent those plays are. Generally speaking, songs that are being played a lot right now will have a higher popularity than songs that were played a lot in the past. Duplicate tracks (e.g., the same track from a single and an album) are rated independently.
